Ingredients

How many people are you cooking for?



Ingredient Amount
Noodles 240g
Olive Oil 2tbsp
Chicken Breast 400g (sliced into chunks)
Green Onion 2 (chopped)
Garlic 4 cloves (crushed)
Carrots 0.5cup
Eggs 2
Brown Sugar 6tbsp
Tamarind Paste 2tbsp
Soy Sauce 1tbsp
Fish Sauce 3tbsp
Lime Juice 1 lime


Method

  • Cook the noodles according to the packet instructions, then rinse in cold water.
  • Make the sauce by qhisking together the sugar, tamarind paste, fish sauce, soy sauce and lime juice. Set aside.
  • Heat half of the oil in a large saucepan or wok over medium-high heat, and saute the chicken (or tofu) for 4-5 minutes, until cooked through.
  • Push the chicken (or tofu) to the side of the pan, and add the remainder of the oil. Add the green onion, carrot and garlic, and saute fo 2 minutes, stirring frequently.
  • Push the vegetables to the side of the pan, and add the eggs. Stir the eggs frequently until cooked through, breaking into small pieces with the spatula.
  • Add the noodles and sauce to the pan and stir to combine.
  • Serve topped with peanuts, sesame seeds, or chilli flakes, with a fresh squeeze of lime.
