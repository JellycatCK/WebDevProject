Ingredients

How many people are you cooking for?



Ingredient Amount
Spaghetti 500g
Olive Oil 2tbsp
Beef Mince 400g
Onion 1 large (chopped)
Garlic 4 cloves (crushed)
Chopped Tomatoes 800g
Beef Stock 400ml
Parmesan 200g


Method

  • Heat half of the olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Cook the mince with a pinch of salt and pepper until dark brown. Transfer to a bowl and set aside.
  • Heat the rest of the olive oil in the same saucepan. Add the onions and cook until translucent; about 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook for another 2 minutes.
  • Add the mince back to the pan, with the tinned tomatoes and stock, and mix well.
  • Simmer with the lid off for around 30 minutes, until reduced to a thick consistency. Taste and season as desired.
  • While the sauce is simmering, bring a large saucepan of water to the boil and cook spaghetti according to packet instructions.
  • Drain the cooked spaghetti, and add to the sauce. Mix well, and serve with fresh basil and parmesan.
